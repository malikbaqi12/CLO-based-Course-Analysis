**1. Upload your result file**
Open the app and look at the left sidebar. Click the upload button and select your course result file (it must be an .xlsx Excel file). Don't worry about settings — the app will automatically figure out the course type, pass marks, CLOs, and everything else on its own.

**2. Change settings only if something looks wrong**
The app sets the pass mark at 60% and CLO threshold at 50% by default. If those don't match your course, just update them from the sidebar sliders. You can also choose how many top and bottom students you want to see (anywhere from 5 to 20).

**3. Browse through the tabs**
Once your file loads, you'll see 6 tabs at the top. Start with Overview for a quick summary, then check Grade Analysis for charts, CLO & GA for attainment results, Risk Analysis to spot failing or struggling students, and Student Data for the full student list.

**4. Search for any student instantly**
Go to the Student Data tab and use the search box to find any student by their name or registration number. Pass students are highlighted in green and fail students in red, so you can spot results at a glance.

**5. Download your reports**
Head to the Export tab when you're done. You can download a CSV file, a full Excel report (with separate sheets for students, CLOs, and GAs), or an HTML dashboard that you can open in any browser even without internet.
